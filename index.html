RINGCENTRAL

Category | 2020-2021
Client:
RingCentral

RingCentral, Inc. is an American publicly traded provider of cloud-based communications and collaboration solutions for businesses RingCentral CEO Vlad Shmunis and CTO Vlad Vendrow founded the company in 1999. RingCentral investors included Doug Leone, Sequoia Capital, David Weiden, Khosla Ventures, Rob Theis, Scale Venture Partners, Bobby Yerramilli-Rao, Hermes Growth Partners and DAG Ventures. It completed its IPO in 2013.

RingCentral provides a cloud-based business phone system. It offers PBX features such as multiple extensions; call control; Outlook, Salesforce, Google Docs, DropBox and Box integration; SMS; video conferencing and web conferencing; fax; auto-receptionist; call logs; and rule-based call routing and answering. Customers do not require capital investment or maintenance contracts, which lowers customer costs and – as with most cloud-based technologies -- "potentially disrupts" traditional on-premises PBX providers.
